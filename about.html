Hello, My Name is Stephen

I'm a UX designer working in the tech field because I have an unending interest in human behavior. I've always been the "observering" type throughout my life, absorbing information and seeing how other people interact with each other. I approach all projects with the scientific method searching for objectivity and truth. My interest for user experience and psychology continues to grow and develop. I believe developing a deep understanding of human and social behavior, and prototyping (a lot) is the key to good product design.

Solving Problems Through Empathy & Research

As a UX designer, I speak for the customer. I love building empathy and stepping into the customer's shoes to understand frustrations. I also dive into the analytical side of UX combing through data, locating patterns and looking for opportunites to improve products.

When I'm Not Working

I enjoy reading old books about philosphy, & cooking pasta dishes.
